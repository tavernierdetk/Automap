# Attribution — samples/frames/

The images in `samples/frames/` are downscaled derivatives of the **Sheffield
Park 3** drone dataset, included here as a tiny test/fallback frame set.

- **Source:** https://github.com/pierotofy/drone_dataset_sheffield_park_3
- **Author:** Piero Toffanin
- **License:** BSD 2-Clause (reproduced below)

The only modification is downscaling to 800px longest edge (JPEG q70) to keep the
repository small.
